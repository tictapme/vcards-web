01 Do I need to install an app?

No, our solution is completely appless. Your vCard is stored in the cloud as a URL that you can save to your desktop for easy access at any time.

02 Who can manage the content of the cards?

Only the administrator(s) have access to centralized content management.

03 Can I use VCards under my own web domain?

Yes, you can use the vCard system under a subdomain of your own. For example, if you own the domain my-company.com, you could access your vCards under any subdomain, such as vcards.my-company.com.

04 Is the NFC Card mandatory?

No, we offer the NFC Card as an optional purchase, costing €6.95 per card (one-time payment). While having the NFC Card is a good option, you can also enjoy Vcards using only the mobile version.

05 Can separate brands be created for different countries or teams?

Yes, you can create different brands for each country, region, or team, and each team can manage their own brand.

06 What advantages does VCards by TicTap have over other solutions?

It’s a completely web-based solution that doesn’t require an application or the support of an IT department. It allows you to edit content in real time without the need for programmers and manage multiple brands and templates in a segmented manner.

07 Can multiple business cards be scanned at once?

Yes, you can scan multiple cards with a single photo and download the contacts to Excel.

08 Can I have the card in different languages?

Yes, the platform supports editing cards in the requested languages.

09 Can the card design be changed?
Yes, you can change the card design, but at an additional cost depending on your needs.
