Cookies policy

What is a cookie?

A cookie is a small piece of information sent by a website and stored in the user’s browser, allowing the user’s previous activity to be consulted. The main objective is to control the users who access the portal as well as to identify what their browsing preferences have been, allowing it to be stored for later consultation. This information is stored on the user’s computer, tablet, or smartphone, and can be updated and retrieved by the web space owner.
The content of a cookie may include the date and time of the visit to the website and the identification of the areas visited by the user while browsing.

Cookies store and retrieve information about the browsing habits of the user or their equipment and, depending on the information they contain and the way in which the user uses their equipment, they can be used to recognise the user, achieving greater personalisation and analysis of the user’s behaviour.
