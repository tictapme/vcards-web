What is a cookie?<\/h3>\n

\nA cookie is a small piece of information sent by a website and stored in the user’s browser, allowing the user’s previous activity to be consulted. The main objective is to control the users who access the portal as well as to identify what their browsing preferences have been, allowing it to be stored for later consultation. This information is stored on the user’s computer, tablet, or smartphone, and can be updated and retrieved by the web space owner.
\nThe content of a cookie may include the date and time of the visit to the website and the identification of the areas visited by the user while browsing.\n <\/p>\n

www.tictap.me<\/a> makes use of its own and\/or third-party cookies in order to provide you with better service and attention through our website, as well as to attend to the services requested via the website efficiently, and to obtain more information about your preferences, personalising the website according to your interests, facilitating efficient and personalised browsing.<\/p>\n

Cookies store and retrieve information about the browsing habits of the user or their equipment and, depending on the information they contain and the way in which the user uses their equipment, they can be used to recognise the user, achieving greater personalisation and analysis of the user’s behaviour.<\/p>\n
