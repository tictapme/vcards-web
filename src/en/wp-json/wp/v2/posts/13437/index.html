Vcards are the digital version of traditional business cards. They are a <\/span>tool that\u2019s turning upside down how we connect with companies and between people<\/b>. <\/span><\/p>\n

They work in a simple way, by means of a QR code on your mobile you share your vcard with whoever you want to connect, for example, a prospect, an investor or a colleague. When the person scans the QR on their mobile, your information and the link to the card are automatically saved in their contact book. You can also <\/span>share it through a link<\/b> by direct messages or email or adding it in your social media profile.<\/span><\/p>\n

Vcards are an ecological and economical solution to the waste of single use paper caused by traditional business cards. If you decide to make the change, you will be adopting a<\/span> sustainable measure that will also save you money and with which you will ensure that your contact information is not lost or forgotten<\/b> as it often happens with many paper business cards. Vcards are also dynamic as they allow you to change the data and have it updated instantly.<\/span><\/p>\n
