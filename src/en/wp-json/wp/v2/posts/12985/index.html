Traditionally, in meetings or networking events came a time when you exchanged your business card with a potential customer, provider or a contact that could be interesting for your work or your business. <\/p>\n

In recent years the digital business card, or digital business card, has appeared. At first there was some suspicion. Therefore, many professionals and companies continued to bet on the card in cardboard or paper of a lifetime. <\/p>\n

One of the barriers mentioned was that the impact created by the paper card was irreplaceable. Of course, for this it was necessary that it had a design and paper very well chosen and according to the brand.\u00a0 <\/p>\n

However, the VOCID-19 pandemic arrived. The tide turned overnight. As a result, events (meetings, fairs, congresses…) have been digitized. So in many causes networking is not done by physical presence but in videoconferences, etc.<\/p>\n\t\t\t\t\t\t<\/div>\n\t\t\t\t<\/div>\n\t\t\t\t

Paper cards have no place in this environment. It is in it precisely where digital business cards take over as a new substitute. <\/p>\n

With an online business card you can continue to share your contact details easily using a QR code, a link, etc. There are different platforms to create your digital business cards. Depending on the platform, you can share them in one way or another. <\/p>\n

Apart from the fact that you will be able to share it in the new environment we are facing due to the pandemic, the online business card has many other advantages to consider. They also have some not so positive aspects or limitations that you should not underestimate.\u00a0 <\/p>\n
